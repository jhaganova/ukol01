Video Games. I highly enjoy exploration, building, and stories in games. I am especially fond of games that offer player choices. Games are also a great way to spend time with people, and if given the option, I always opt for a co-op game rather than something competitive.

Reading. I absolutely love stories. This makes me drawn to books like a moth to a flame. I prefer Young Adult books, especially fantasy and romance genre. My current favourite series are the Raven Boys series, and the Six of Crows duology.

Party Games and Board Games. Great way to spend time with family and friends, whether one wants to relax, be challenged, or just be completely silly and have a laugh.

Writing and Drawing. I write stories, RP, poems/lyrics, and sometimes I sketch characters. Personally, I think it's fun to explore characters, their journeys and experience, their emotions and their growth, while being at the epicentre of it.

Travel. It is enriching to travel and see new places. My favourite trips are to nature. I like to be surrounded by the greenery and the quiet; I love hills and mountains; I find it absolutely wonderful to feel the enormity of the world around me, detached from the everyday hustle and bustle.

Photography. I like taking photos, capturing a moment into something lasting. I try to make photos from various trips look nice. I also try to take photos that matter to me—snapshot memories to look back on to.

Dogs. Who could resist? ♥

Hangouts. Taking time out of the day to put the screen away and simply be with people can be so precious. I like evening talks out in a pub, winding-down with shisha in hand, or using the time together to interact beyond a conversation—billiards, badminton, laser games.

...Coding? Only time will tell! ;)
